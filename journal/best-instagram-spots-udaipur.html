7 most Instagrammable spots at The Udaisarovar

Udaipur is the most photogenic city in India — and just outside it, on the water at Lakadwas, our lakeside resort has quietly become one of the most Instagrammable stays around. Here are the seven shots every guest goes home with.

1. The infinity pool at the lake's edge

Our pool spills straight into the horizon, with palms and the Aravalli hills behind. Shoot it at golden hour for a mirror-still reflection.

2. Twin palms at sunrise

The two lone palms on the shore, wrapped in fairy lights, framed against morning mist over the water — this is the Udaisarovar photo.

3. Bonfire nights

Guitars, drums and a fire under a sky full of stars. Long-exposure sparks make magic.

4. The red-roof cottages

Our cottage row against the lake and hills — warm wood, red roofs, big skies.

5. Lakeside camping tents

Green tents on the grass by the water with the palace lights shimmering across the lake at night.

6. Cinema under the stars

A projector on the shore, the lake glowing behind — one of the most-shared setups we do.

7. Home-plated food, lakeside

Fresh Rajasthani thalis on marble tables with the lake as the backdrop. Flat-lays welcome.
