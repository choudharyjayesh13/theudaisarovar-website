The parties we throw by the lake

The Udaisarovar isn't only for quiet weekends — it's one of Udaipur's most versatile lakeside event venues. With full production, sound and décor by JD Production & Entertainers and Pronite, we've hosted celebrations of every kind on the water.

Lakeside weddings & phere

Floral mandaps at blue hour, garden ceremonies and full setups for up to 150 guests — with the lake and hills as your backdrop. Packages start from ₹1,00,000, or ₹1,50,000 with catering.

Holi & festival bashes

Our Holi party is legendary — a big canopy, a DJ, a rain of colour and the cottages framing the dance floor. Sundowners, foam and bubble pool parties, and craziest-sundowner setups with DJs flown in from Jaipur and Mumbai.

DJ & sundowner nights

Production-grade lights, sound and stage on the shore, artists curated by Pronite, and the palace lights of Udaipur shimmering across the water. New Year's Eve here is unforgettable.

Birthdays, baby showers & corporate

Balloon arches, stages, catering and bartenders — handled end to end. Corporate offsites get guarded parking, power backup and a full event team.
