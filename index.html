Why The Udaisarovar

A lakeside hotel in Udaipur that feels like your own

Looking for the best lakeside hotel in Udaipur without the five-star price tag? The Udaisarovar sits right on the water at Lakadwas, a short drive from Udaipur's City Palace and Fateh Sagar — close enough for sightseeing, far enough for real peace and open sky.

We're one of the most reasonable lakeside stays in Udaipur: private lake-view cottages start at just ₹3,000, and you can take the entire villa for a group from ₹18,000. Every stay comes with an infinity pool on the lakefront, bonfire evenings, lakeside tented camping and home-cooked Rajasthani food — the kind of Udaipur experience the big hotels can't match.
